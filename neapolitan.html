Neapolitan Style

Neapolitan pizza is the classic: soft, airy crust with leopard spotting, simple toppings, and a very hot bake. It’s usually light, quick, and focused on quality ingredients.

Quick Tips

  • Keep toppings minimal (tomato, mozzarella, basil is the blueprint).
  • High heat is key—use your oven’s hottest setting.
  • Let the dough ferment to build flavor and softness.
