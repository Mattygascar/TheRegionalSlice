Neapolitan Style

Neapolitan pizza is the classic Italian style known for its soft crust, minimal toppings, and extremely hot bake.

Quick Tips

  • Use simple toppings like tomato, mozzarella, and basil.
  • Bake at the highest temperature possible.
  • Allow dough to ferment to build flavor.
