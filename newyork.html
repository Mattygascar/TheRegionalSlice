New York Style

New York–style pizza is known for large foldable slices with a thin-to-medium crust, a crisp underside, and a chewy bite.

Quick Tips

  • Stretch dough thin with a slightly thicker rim.
  • Bake hot and fast.
  • Keep toppings light so slices remain foldable.

NY Slice Basics Table

Feature Typical NY Style Why It Matters
Crust Thin to medium, foldable Easy to eat by the slice
Cheese Low moisture mozzarella Good melt without sogginess
