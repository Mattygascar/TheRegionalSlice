Detroit Style

Detroit-style pizza is a thick, airy square pizza baked in a pan. The signature move is the crispy cheese edge—cheese caramelizes against the pan creating a crunchy border.

What Makes It Different

  • Square pan-baked pizza
  • Cheese goes all the way to the edges
  • Sauce is often added on top
