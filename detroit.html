Detroit Style

Detroit-style pizza is a thick, airy square pizza baked in a pan. The signature move is the crispy “frico” edge—cheese that caramelizes against the pan—plus sauce often added on top.

What Makes It Different

  • Square shape (pan-baked)
  • Cheese to the edges for crispy corners
  • Sauce frequently layered on top after baking
